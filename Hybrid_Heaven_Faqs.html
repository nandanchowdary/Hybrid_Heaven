Each plant comes with care instructions. Generally:

  • Water when the top inch of soil is dry
  • Provide adequate sunlight as per plant requirements
  • Use well-draining soil and pots with drainage holes
  • Fertilize during growing season (spring-summer)

Check the specific care tag that came with your plant for detailed instructions.
